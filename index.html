About

I’m Jeewan Adhikari, a Civil Engineering graduate specializing in water resources. I work on flood hazard mapping, hydrologic/hydraulic simulation, and climate scenario analysis to support resilient infrastructure planning.

I enjoy combining physically-based models (e.g., HEC-RAS/HEC-HMS) with data science (Python) and geospatial workflows (GIS).

Data-driven hydrology

Machine learning for rainfall–runoff, bias correction, and probabilistic prediction.
